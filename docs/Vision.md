# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

To provide public managers with clear, evidence-based insights into the effectiveness and social impact of cultural programs, enabling data-informed decisions, greater transparency, and continuous improvement of public policies.

---

## The Problem

Public cultural programs are often evaluated using operational indicators such as budget execution, municipalities served, and participant counts.

While useful, these metrics do not measure whether the program generated meaningful social impact.

---

## The Solution

This project proposes a Business Intelligence dashboard that transforms citizen perception surveys into actionable indicators capable of supporting evidence-based decision making.

---

## Target Users

- Public Managers
- Government Agencies
- Cultural Departments
- Researchers
- Data Analysts

---

## Core Value

Transform survey responses into meaningful public policy indicators.
