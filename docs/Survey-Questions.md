# Survey Questions

## Overview

This document defines the survey questionnaire used to evaluate the effectiveness of the **Cineleitura do Bem** program.

The survey is intended to be completed by the municipal public agency responsible for coordinating or supporting the program after its conclusion.

The questionnaire is divided into three sections:

1. General Information
2. Program Evaluation
3. Suggestions
